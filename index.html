How Clique Works

Getting started with Clique is easy and fun. Here's a quick guide to connecting with other crews.

1

Form Your Clique

Invite your friends to create your unique Clique profile. Highlight your group's interests, hobbies, and what makes your crew awesome!

2

Discover Other Cliques

Explore groups near you with similar interests. Our intelligent matching algorithm suggests Cliques you'll genuinely connect with.

3

Match & Connect

When there's a mutual interest, it's a match! Chat with other Cliques, plan meetups, and expand your social circle together.

About Clique

In a world increasingly connected digitally, we noticed a gap in how people form real-life, meaningful connections with their friends. Traditional social apps focus on individual connections, often leading to awkward first meets or fizzled friendships. That's why we created Clique: an app that champions group-to-group interaction.

Our mission is to foster genuine community by enabling friends to expand their social circles together. We believe the best experiences are shared, and with Clique, you can easily discover other like-minded groups for shared adventures, new hobbies, or just a great night out. We're committed to building a platform that's fun, safe, and truly enhances your social life.
